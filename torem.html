Torem

An Android mobile application to create, share, and explore travel plans created by real users and help ease the process of travelling in unknown or now places. This project was created as a capstone project of Bangkit 2021.

Torem was developed by a team of 6 people, divided equally from 3 learning paths which are Cloud Computing, Mobile Development, and Machine Learning.
