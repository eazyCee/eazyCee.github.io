Bangkit 2021

for 6 months in 2021, I enrolled in Bangkit 2021, a program initiated by Google in collaboration with Gojek, Traveloka, and Tokopedia that aims to develop tech talents in Indonesia through creating projects and online classes. Out of almost 28,000 registrants, 3000 were selected to enroll and thankfully I was one of them. It was a rigorous process of continued learning and implementation, and at the end of the program we were tasked to create a capstone project that encompasses everything we've learned during the project, which will be peer reviewed and judged. My team and I decided to create a tourism app called 'Torem' that aims to recover Indonesia's falling tourism sector caused by the pandemic.
